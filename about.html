About Me

What I do and facts about me.

Short Bio

I was born in Morocco in April 1992. At the age of six years old I moved to Italy and after 10 years I got the Italian citizenship. I spent more than 17 years in Parma, a lovely city in the northern part of Italy. I graduated in Computer Science at the University of Parma, Department of Mathematics in April 2016. At the end of the third year of my course of study I moved to London. After graduation I moved from London to the south west of England, Wiltshire, where I worked as a Software Developer with a Google Partner company using the Google Cloud Platform. After 1 year and 2 months in the UK I returned to Parma and I currently work in Milan as a Software Developer.


What I love to do

I spend almost 2 hours and 40 minutes in train to go to work and return home, so I use this time to Read, Listen, Code and Watch the sunrise in the morning. When I'm not in a train or in a office and is not winter I usually go for a bike-ride in the countryside.
