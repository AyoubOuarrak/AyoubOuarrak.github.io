About Me

A brief biography until now

Ayoub's timeline

Beginning

  • Ayoub's birth

    8 April 1992

    I was born in Morocco, in a small village not too far from Casablanca.

  • Left Morocco

    September 1999

    At age of 6, I left Morocco and moved to Italy (Parma) with my family for a new beginning. I remember very well this moments, not the happiest memories I have.

  • Primary school

    2000 - 2007

    Primary school was not easy, I never felt integrated and welcomed. Memories are blurred for this period...

  • High school

    2007 - 2012

    High school was less painful, is in this period that I discovered Computer Programming.

  • University

    October 2012

    After graduating from High School in Computer Science, I continued my path starting the course of Computer Science in the University of Parma, Department of Mathematics.

  • Moved to UK

    September 2015

    I started in this period preparing my last exam: "Semantics of programming languages". Also in this period I moved to London to prepare my thesis on the subject of Cryptography.

  • Graduation

    26 April 2016

    After finalizing my thesis, I returned to Parma for the big day

  • First Job, Wiltshire

    28 April 2016

    After graduation (2 days after) I moved from London to Swindon, Wiltshire where I started working for Appsbroker as a Java Software Developer. I met extraordinary people and got addicted to Tea.

  • Left UK

    November 2016

    I returned to Italy (for family reasons) and started working in Milan as a Software Developer.

  • Work in Luxembourg, EP

    July 2017

    Left Italy permanently and moved In Metz, France. In August I started working for the European Parliament in Luxembourg as an External Software Engineer.

  • Work in Luxembourg, PwC

    September 2018 - Today

    After one year working for the European Parliament, I left the life of consultant and joined PwC where I work as an Analyst Developer.
